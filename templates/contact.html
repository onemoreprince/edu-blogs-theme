Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Do you accept guest posts, paid posts, or backlink requests?
No. We do not accept guest posts, sponsored posts, paid backlinks, link exchanges, or any similar requests.

2. What is the purpose of this website?
This site is designed to help college students quickly find clear, structured, and easy-to-navigate study resources for various subjects and courses.

3. Who writes the content?
Our content is created and refined by a team of contributors, which may include multiple writers, ghostwriters, and AI-assisted writing/editing tools. Because of this collaborative workflow, we do not list a single dedicated author.
We reference a wide range of books and online materials, and citations are included wherever possible (with more being added over time).

4. Can I reuse content from this website?
Yes, you may quote or use partial snippets with proper credit and a link back to our website. Full republication of articles is not permitted.

5. Are you open to business or collaboration opportunities?
No. We are not open to business pitches, partnership requests, or any commercial opportunities.
